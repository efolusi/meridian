Square icon-only button for toolbars, card actions, and dense rows; `label` is required (a11y + tooltip).

```jsx
<IconButton icon="pencil" label="Edit" />
<IconButton icon="ellipsis" label="More" variant="outline" size="sm" />
```

Variants: `quiet` (default, transparent), `outline`, `solid` (espresso ink). Sizes `sm|md|lg`.
