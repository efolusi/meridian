# UsageMeter

Budget meter: used/limit figures, fill bar that turns warning past warnAt and danger past the limit.

`<UsageMeter label="Tokens this month" used={812000} limit={1000000} unit="tokens" cost="$4.82" hint="Resets Aug 1" />`

Use for token, run, or spend budgets in sidebars and billing. For task progress use Progress instead.
